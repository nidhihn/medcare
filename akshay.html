Cardiac Surgeries

Cardiac surgeries are intricate procedures addressing heart conditions. Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG) involves rerouting blood around blocked arteries, while valve replacement corrects faulty heart valves. Angioplasty employs a balloon to open narrowed arteries, often accompanied by stent placement to maintain blood flow.These surgeries aim to enhance cardiac health and treat cardiovascular disorders.

Orthopedic Surgeries

Orthopedic surgeries involve the diagnosis and treatment of musculoskeletal conditions, including bones, joints, ligaments, muscles, and tendons. These procedures address issues such as fractures, joint disorders, arthritis, and deformities,these procedures aim to improve the function and structure of the musculoskeletal system for enhanced mobility and overall well-being.

Neurosurgery

Neurosurgery involves surgical interventions on the nervous system, including the brain and spinal cord, to treat conditions like tumors, trauma, or neurological disorders for improved patient health by precise and specialized procedures that treat disorders affecting the delicate structures of the nervous system.

Gastrointestinal Surgery

Gastrointestinal surgery focuses on the digestive system, treating conditions like hernias, colorectal disorders, and stomach issues through surgical procedures for improved gastrointestinal health,providing effective solutions for disorders affecting the organs involved in digestion, absorption, and nutrient processing within the digestive tract.

Ophthalmic Surgery

Ophthalmic surgery focuses on procedures related to the eyes, treating conditions such as cataracts, glaucoma, and refractive errors. Surgeons aim to improve vision and address eye-related disorders through interventions like lens replacement and laser surgeries, enhancing the overall health and functionality of the eyes.

Dental Surgery

Involves procedures related to the teeth and oral cavity.Tooth extraction, root canal, and dental implants are common dental surgeries.These surgeries aim to restore oral health, alleviate pain, and enhance the functionality and aesthetics of the teeth and surrounding structures.
